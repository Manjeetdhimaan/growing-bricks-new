Haridwar Greens

Located in the City Of Gods, Haridwar Greens is nothing short of a paradise. Conceptualized and designed by the globally renowned B+H Architects from Canada, this township seamlessly blends the enchanting views of the Shivalik Hills, the tranquillity of nature and the comforts of a new age living to give you an unparalleled experience. Placed in an area which undergoing massive infrastructural development, Haridwar Greens offers uninterrupted connectivity to all major landmarks including Har Ki Pauri, SIDCUL, Patanjali Yogpeeth, the International and Domestic airport.
