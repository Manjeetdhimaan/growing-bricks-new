Alaknanda

A dream destination for anyone looking to build their dream home, Alaknanda by Hero Realty is situated at a prime location on the Delhi-Dehradun highway (NH-58) and is surrounded by lush greenery. This extensive project of 88.93 acres is one of Haridwar’s freehold townships that offers all modern day amenities. Ideal for people looking for a second home or a holiday home, its close proximity to Patanjali Yogpeeth and connectivity to popular hill stations is an added advantage.
